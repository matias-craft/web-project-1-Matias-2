What is Solo Leveling?

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, the world's weakest hunter, who gains the ability to level up in strength and skill through a mysterious system.

Setting

The story is set in a world where hunters, individuals with special abilities, fight monsters that invade from dungeons. The narrative explores various locations, including dungeons, cities, and the hunter's guild.

Overview

Solo Leveling is a captivating tale of growth, determination, and the struggle against overwhelming odds. It combines elements of fantasy, action, and adventure, making it a must-read for fans of the genre.
